assignment description: sketch 8 different ideas for enhancing an ai tool (not just “add a button lol”), then pick one direction and flesh it out into a lo-fi task flow for a real user task.
<br><br>
for this one we had to stay away from the usual ui homework (login screens, keyboards, text boxes) and instead think about how an ai chat app could fit into someone’s actual day.
<br><br>
so i treated gpt like a slightly unhinged operating system: it has files, folders, a to‑do list, timestamps, even a “brain dump” widget that just listens while you talk. these are the eight sketches:
